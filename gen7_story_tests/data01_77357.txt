Once upon a time, there was a little generous boy named Tim. There was also a little needy girl named Sue. There was also a magic tree. Tim met Sue. Her toy broke. Tim felt sad. He had an idea. He fixed her broken toy. He wanted help from Tree. Tree promised to turn the penny into her toy. Tim gave the penny to Tree. The penny turned into her toy. He received her toy. He gave her toy to Sue. Sue felt happy. Tim and Sue played together. Tim and Sue became good friends. He learned that helping a friend could bring joy.
