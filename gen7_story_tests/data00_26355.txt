Once upon a time, there was a playful duck named Ducky. There was also a helpful resourceful frog named Froggy. Every day, Ducky played with friends in the pond. It lost its toy at the bottom of the pond and felt sad. It asked Froggy to retrieve the lost toy. Froggy found a simple hook. It searched around the pond. It found the stick. It found the string. It made its hook. Ducky felt happy.
