Once upon a time, there was a playful duck named Ducky. There was also a helpful resourceful frog named Froggy. Every day, Ducky played with friends in the pond. Ducky lost its toy at the bottom of the pond and felt sad. Ducky asked Froggy to retrieve the lost toy. Froggy found a simple hook. Froggy searched around the pond. Froggy found the stick. Froggy found the string. Froggy made its hook. Froggy used the hook to retrieve the lost toy. Ducky felt happy. Ducky thanked Froggy.
