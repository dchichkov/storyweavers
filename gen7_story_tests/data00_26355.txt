Once upon a time, there was a playful duck named Ducky. There was also a helpful resourceful frog named Froggy. Every day, Ducky played with friends. It lost its toy near the bottom and felt sad. It felt sad. It asked Froggy to retrieve the lost toy. Froggy found a simple hook. It searched around the pond. It found the stick. It found the string. It made its hook. Ducky felt happy.
